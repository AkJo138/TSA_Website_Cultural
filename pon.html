Pontiac's Rebellion Tour

February 19 12:30 PM - 2:00 PM

Age Group: 10+ Cost: 30$

Overview

This landmark event in American history between a large group of Native Americans and British soldiers is the subject of our second tour, which will take you through recountings, battles, and the culmination of the war, ending on a meditative reflection on the effects of this war, such as the Proclamation of 1763. An attendee of this tour will gain a greater appreciation of the influence that Native Americans had on the development of modern American society and government.
