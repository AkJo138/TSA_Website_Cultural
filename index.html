The Native American Heritage Museum Tour

Our History

The history of the United States is imbued with the arrival and preservation of cultures from all around the world. However, the original cultures tend to be forgotten in the amalgamation of heritage. Since 1978, our chapter has been dedicated the preserve the heritage of the Native American cultures that once engrossed the American landscape. We display such heritage by offering our virtual tours through our numerous exhibits of different tribes and interactive activites to engage them in the Native American culture.
