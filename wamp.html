Wampanoag Tribe Tour

February 19 10:00 AM - 11:00 AM

Age Group: 5+ Cost: 25$

Overview

This interactive tour will take you through the history of the Wampanoags, which were a group of Native American tribes along the East Coast of the United States with a rich history. This tour will start by portraying the pre-colonization traditions of the Wampanoag, followed by the increasing conflict between European settlers, culminating in the King Philip's War, with the tour ending on the present day status of the tribe.
