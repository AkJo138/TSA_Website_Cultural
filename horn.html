Battle of Little Bighorn Tour

February 19 19 1:00 PM - 3:00 PM

Age Group: 10+ Cost: 25$

Overview

Shifting to the post-Civil War era of American history, this tour focuses on an 1876 military conflict between the Cheyenne, Sioux, and Arapaho tribes and American soldiers over the wrongful application of a signed treaty. This tour will take you through a visual, auditory, and emotional examination of this rare defeat for the United States army at the hands of these tribes, and heavy focus will be given to the aftermath of this battle.
