Tlingit Tribe Tour

February 19 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Age Group: 5+ Cost: 25$

Overview

Advancing westward, the subjects of this tour are the Tlingits, who are the inhabitants of the state of Alaska. The animist religion of the Tlingits will be a focus, with an emphasis on the totem poles and architectural endeavors they constructed. There will be an examination of the effect of the introduction of the Christian faith by colonizers on this tribe, and corollaries with how advancing religion and forcing it upon different people can lead to cultural homogenization and loss.
